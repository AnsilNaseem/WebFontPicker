February 2013

Robomac 2013

Robomac is an annually organised event where students build robots and attend lectures on robotics. For a week, students work in teams on a category of their choice and present their robots on last day of the event.

My role was to design and code this year’s event site, using the site and content from the previous year. I started the project by analysing last year’s site and determining how it could be improved. Some of the changes I made include:

  • Showing the different stages of the event and their deadlines
  • Reorganising and restructuring the content and aligning it to a grid
  • Adding important information about the event to the homepage
